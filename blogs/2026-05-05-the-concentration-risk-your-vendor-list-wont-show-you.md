A category can look well-diversified on the approved vendor list while one supplier quietly absorbs most of the actual spend. By the time the pattern shows up in a quarterly review, the contract is often close to renewal and the leverage has already moved. That is not a sourcing failure. It is a visibility failure. Category spend reports show totals.
